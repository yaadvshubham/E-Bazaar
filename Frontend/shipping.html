Taxes & GST Logic

At E-Bazaar, all prices displayed on our product pages and cart are Maximum Retail Price (MRP) inclusive of all taxes. You will never see surprise GST charges added on top of your final bill at checkout.

As per Indian GST norms, your final invoice will contain a breakdown of the Base Price and GST charged based on the product category:

  • 5% GST: Apparel & Clothing (Under ₹1000), Essential Groceries.
  • 12% GST: Apparel & Shoes (Above ₹1000), Packaged Foods.
  • 18% GST: Electronics, Gadgets, Beauty & Cosmetics.
  • 0% GST: Books and Unprocessed Groceries.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your package directly from your Orders page.

International Shipping

Currently, E-Bazaar only ships within India. We are working hard to expand our logistics network globally soon!
